Background & Personal Life

Finn Russell is known not only for his rugby talent but also for his relaxed, creative personality off the pitch. Before becoming a professional player, he worked as a stonemason while playing club rugby in Scotland.

He is widely admired for his leadership, humour, and ability to stay calm under pressure — traits that make him one of Scotland’s most iconic players.
